MIDI Composition

Info! (Please pay attention to turn down the volume)
Mp3 Version:

The piece was written two years ago when my first band in college disbanded. Two of our team members graduated and might not be able to by our side. The emotion expressed here is the strong power of friendship. Even if we face difficulties in our lives, as long as we have friends by our sides, we will be able to overcome the hardships.

An interesting part about this MIDI arrangement is that I vastly used the humanize function to make the original machine-like neat into slightly irregular so that it has kind of feeling like played by real humans.

Musique Concrète Project

Mp3 Version:

This is an audio collage project focused on creative listening, consisting of a variety of sound clips. Those clips intentionally to be quite short - all of them are less than two seconds. Many interesting things can happen in two seconds, and it's even more interesting to put clips of sounds within two seconds together in such a way that their sum creates something entirely new.

I collected sounds from animals, martial art movies, and video games, and trying to depict a story of a fight between two Ninjas. The scene happened at midnight in the wild, surrounded by insects and wolves. In the first round, one Ninja is chasing another, and the chaser continues to shoot the escaper with darts. Then the escaper was hit by a dart and get injured, fell to the ground. The chaser was quite complacent. In the second round, the escaper had to fight with the chaser. After two battles, one person was eventually killed and the other won. (Who wins and who loses is depends on your guess.)
